Best Halloween events in Ireland 2021

Halloween Spooktacular at Irish National Stud and Gardens, Co. Kildare – for a full day of fun

From spooky crafts to storytelling, magic shows, and more, there is plenty of festive fun to be had here between 23 and 31 October.

Crumlin Road Gaol Halloween Jail of Horror, Co. Antrim – not for the faint-hearted

Are you brave enough to undertake a paranormal investigation? Or maybe you’d rather discover the so-called ‘Jail of Horrors’? Whichever you choose, these are certainly among the most terrifying Halloween events on offer.

BOOK: HERE!

Halloween at Hillsborough Castle, Co. Down – discover the Castle’s spooky history

For a family-friendly activity, book onto the spooky Wild Raven Walk. This tour will take you on a magical walk around the gardens and discover the spooky side of Hillsborough Castle.

BOOK: HERE!

Halloween at Fota House and Gardens, Co. Cork – fun for all the family

Spookfest is an hour-long event involving dancing, games, and lots of Halloween fun from 23 to 25 October. Or, you can take a self-guided Halloween Ramble around the Arboretum and Victorian Working Gardens.

BOOK: HERE!

Púca Festival, Co. Louth and Co. Meath – paying homage to Halloween’s Celtic origins

Returning to the Ancient East this Halloween is Ireland’s famous Púca Festival. Paying homage to the Celtic tradition of Samhain, the festival will involve lots of music, fire, feasting, and merriment.

BOOK: HERE!

Farmaphobia 2021, Co. Meath – one of the best Halloween events in Ireland this year

Ireland’s biggest Halloween event, it is described as ‘four terrifying haunts spread over 100 acres of fear’, such as WWII Zombies, Carnevil, Purge Night, and Momma’s Murder Maze, this is an event not to be missed.

BOOK: HERE!

The Nightmare Realm, Co. Dublin – strictly for the adults

Voted ‘Best Independent Haunt in Europe 2020’ by Scare Tour and Unilad Adventure, With a multitude of spooky events taking place across the city, this haunting event is strictly adults only.

BOOK: HERE!

Aboo Halloween Festival, Co. Galway – Halloween in the Capital of Culture

Ireland’s Capital of Culture comes to life at Halloween with the Aboo Halloween Festival. Visitors can marvel at the terrifying Macnas Halloween Parade and take part in lots of fun and free events taking place across the weekend.

BOOK: HERE!

Derry Halloween, Co. Derry – the biggest Halloween festival in Europe

The biggest Halloween festival in Europe! From the terrifying events included in Awakening the Walled City to the breathtaking firework show, this is sure to be a Halloween you’ll never forget.
